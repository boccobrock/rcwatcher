'''''Natasha''''' is a EP by [[Pig Destroyer]], released in 2008 by Relapse Records. The EP consists of one song, simply named [[Natasha]], originally included on special editions of ''[[Terrifyer]]'' as a DVD. The song is 37:55 in length, and consists of multiple songs and audio samples. Unlike Pig Destroyer's traditional grindcore sound, this showcases a doom, drone and dark ambient side of Pig Destroyer.
